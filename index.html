Vision

Our vision is to unite and empower individuals in the world of cryptocurrency by providing a trusted and vibrant community which offers profitable investments and fosters education. We strive to be the go-to platform for crypto enthusiasts who want to achieve financial independence and navigate the ever-evolving crypto landscape.

Mission

Our mission is to create the best crypto-related community in Romania with NFT-based access. We plan to provide reliable trading signals, comprehensive market analysis, and educational resources, all to enable individuals to make informed investment decisions. We also want to create a thriving decentralised autonomous organisation - DAO - which will actively invest in promising crypto projects. We plan to achieve realistic milestones and generate consistent monthly profits for our members.

Motives

We provide a dynamic hub where members can connect, learn, share insights, and capitalize on the immense potential of the crypto market. Through transparent governance and inclusive decision-making, we offer an environment of trust, collaboration and shared success, as well as generate monthly profits for our DAO members. We aim to shape the future of crypto trading, setting new standards of excellence and integrity.

Diamond Pass

The "Diamond" NFTs represent the pinnacle of access and privileges within our investments and trading community. These rare NFTs can be obtained exclusively through the burn process, either by burning 5 "Gold" NFTs or by burning 4 "Gold" NFTs and one "Silver" NFT.
In addition to complete access to all our Discord server channels, you will also receive a seat in the Diamond Council. As a "Diamond" NFT holder, you will be among the first to learn about the ideas discussed and decisions made within our council. You will have the opportunity to actively participate in the decision-making processes and contribute to shaping our community.
Another significant advantage is your entitlement to a share of the BaniDAO treasury. As a member of our community and a "Diamond" NFT holder, you will passively benefit from "Bani Gratis" based on the performance and success of our treasury.
